Fitness Classes

Let's Move offers three different kinds of fitness classes: Barre, Kickboxing, and Pilates.

Barre

This fitness class is designed to strengthen your body in a way that combines elements from ballet, yoga, and pilates through low-impact, high-intensity movements. A barre class is typically a full-body workout, but sometimes a certain muscle group may be the particular focus of the class. This is achieved through the awareness of muscle groups during small, controlled movements.

Our Barre instructor: Michaela Blake

Kickboxing

As indicated by the name, this combat sport combines techniques from boxing in addition to kicking, blocking, and kneeing. Participants in this class can expect a warm-up, followed by shadow-boxing and pad work, and finished off with a session of stretching. Kickboxing has been shown to help with cardiovascular strength, as well as providing self-defense skills.

Our Kickboxing instructor: Trevor Barker

Pilates

Pilates is a form of low-impact exercise that focuses on strengthening the muscles (especially the core) and improving your body alignment, posture, and flexibility. The exercises and movements in this class typically use your bodyweight to tone and strenghten your body.

Our Pilates instructor: Thomas Olson
