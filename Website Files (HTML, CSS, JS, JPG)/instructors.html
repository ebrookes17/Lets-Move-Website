Our Instructors

Trevor Barker

Born in Edmonton, AB, Trevor was introduced to kickboxing in his teen years and almost instantly became a dedicated student. Now, at the age of twenty-six, he spends the majority of his time practicing his technique and refining his teaching style in a way that motivates his students. Trevor's kickboxing classes are an exhilarating experience which have been a student-favourite since he joined us last year.

Trevor instructs the following: Kickboxing

Michaela Blake

Fun, fit, and fearless, Michaela leads her barre students with an attitude that inspires to you succeed. As an instructor with Let's Move for the past four years, she continues to lead exciting barre classes week after week.

Michaela instructs the following: Barre

Arif Chandra

First exposed to the disciplined practice of Kundalini yoga at the age of fifteen, Arif strives for a sense of spiritual awakening in every facet of his life. He guides his students through the practice with a sharp attention detail and a disciplined approach that is neccessary to progress in Kundalini yoga. Arif has been teaching with us for the past two years.

Arif instructs the following: Kundalini yoga

Pranita Dhaliwal

Pranita is originally from Agra, India, and has been refining her practice of Vinyasa yoga for over 7 years. You can always expect a refreshing mix of poses and sequences in Pranita's Vinyasa yoga class, as well as her cheerful, encouraging demeanor to go with it. Pranita has been an instructor with Let's Move for the past five years.

Pranita instructs the following: Vinyasa yoga

Sandra May

Energetic, cheerful, and attentive to detail, Sandra is the ideal hip-hop dance instructor. Every class with her has been full of energy and enthusiasm since she started teaching with us nine years ago. Whether you are new to Hip-Hop dance or an expert, Sandra will find a way for you to fit right in and have a blast.

Sandra instructs the following: Hip-Hop dance

Lucia Mcdermott

A student of ballet since the age of six, Lucia intends to share her love of the elegant practice with as many people as she can. Originally from Vancouver, BC, Lucia moved to Kamloops three years ago and ever since has been teaching ballet with Let's Move. Her teaching style is laid back and relaxed, encouraging her students through gentle suggestions and feedback.

Lucia instructs the following: Ballet

Thomas Olson

A fitness enthusiast nearly his whole life, Thomas joined our team four months ago with the goal of teaching the best pilates class he possibly can. Always pushing students to reach for the sky, Thomas' supportive approach will surely inspire you to push yourself to your limits.

Thomas instructs the following: Pilates

Holly Quinn

Since she fell in love with the practice of Yin yoga at the age of twenty-three, Holly swears by her routine of at least one hour of Yin yoga every day. Guiding you deeper and deeper into a relaxed state of mind, her soothing voice and composed vibe will leave you feeling refreshed and awakened after class. Holly has been teaching with us for the past seven years.

Holly instructs the following: Yin yoga

Lang Tai

Lang Tai comes to us from Shenzhen, China and has been a part of the Let's Move team for the past 9 months. Incorporating styles and techniques she has picked up from her travels all over the world, her Fusion dance class is a unique experience not to be missed. Every class with Lang will make you forget about the rest of your week and just simply dance!

Lang instructs the following: Fusion dance
