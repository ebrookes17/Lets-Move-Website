Dance Classes

Let's Move offers classes for three different kinds of dance: Ballet, Fusion, and Hip-Hop.

Ballet

Our ballet class is an introduction to the proper alignment and movements in Ballet all while maintaining a casual and light atmosphere. Ballet shoes are not required, and participants are encouraged to simply have fun. This class is aimed at beginner-to-novice level students.

Our Ballet instructor: Lucia Mcdermott

Fusion

As the name suggests, Fusion dance borrows elements from several different dance styles and combines them into one. Some of these dance styles include Krumping, Polynesian, Hip Hop, Stomp, Belly Dance which are blended together in a fun, fluid way. This is a great class for introductory dance students as it will give you a taste of several different dance styles.

Our Fusion dance instructor: Lang Tai

Hip-Hop

This high-energy Hip-Hop dance class differs from other dance styles because it is often improvisational in nature. Participants dance to upbeat music often in a quick and energetic way. Some older dance styles that were incorporated into Hip-Hop dance are funk, breaking, and uprock.

Our Hip-Hop dance instructor: Sandra May
