About Us

Let's Move Studio has been running for over 20 years, making it the longest-running studio in Kamloops. We offer a large variety of classes to help you achieve your fitness goals and personal well-being. Whether its a graceful ballet class, an invigorating kickboxing class, or a soothing yoga class, we have a little something for everyone. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced students are all welcome, because every class at Let's Move is designed with an all-levels approach in mind. Our instructors are friendly, fun, and most importantly, they genuinely care about your goals and personal safety as a student. If you are looking for an effective, refreshing way to get moving, come join us and let's get moving together!
